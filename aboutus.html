ABOUT US

Our Story

iDeal Technologies L.L.C. started as a project in collaboration with an Austrian company, AVDM GmbH. By using our physical and intellectual assets, we are able to provide the best service for your company. iDeal Technologies is an IT outsourcing company based in Kosovo. Our team is built to offer only the best services for our clients. Together with our partners, we managed to provide quality IT-services to businesses, organizations, and institutions. iDeal Technologies is a one-stop solution for all you IT needs. iDealTechnologies has developed the Data Management Framework to obtain a uniform view of the data, to process it in a quality-assured, clear, transparent and traceable manner. We support you in the conception, implementation, and ongoing readjustment of your individual data management strategy with the aim of a data-driven organization.
Whether it is upgrading your current website or creating a new one, we have the expertise to do so. Software product development changes continually and you must stay on top of the changes to keep your business booming.
By having multiple offices in the Balkans and in Central Europe, we have created the perfect network to be available for our clients.
Our specialization gives you the security that we will not deal with anything else, day or night. That is why we are equipped with know-how in this area that you will hardly find anywhere else in this form.

Mission

As a technology company, our mission is to create the conditions for solving our customers' business challenges, problems and needs, by providing the demanded products and top-notch service. We will develop seamless business solutions, promote our clients’ innovative products, create jobs for the talented minds, and global offices to boost interaction.

Vision

We see our company as the central point of contact for Software Development and Data Management who redefines innovation leadership. As a top employer, we develop our employees at the highest level. As a future-oriented global player, we recognize and promote creativity and out-of-the-box thinking. Tackling and solving is our daily activity. This results in stable and strong growth.

Our Partner

AVDM supports your company in solving your current information technology challenges and accompanies you into the digital future. We are data managers and infrastructure specialists who work in the three main business areas such as data management, quality assurance and infrastructure. Our experts work closely with your company to quickly deliver individual and practice-oriented solutions. In the course of our 11 years of experience in the Austrian market, we have looked after over 250 customers and established ourselves as the central contact for data management.
