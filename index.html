About us

iDeal Technologies is an IT outsourcing company based in Kosovo. Our team is built to offer only the best services for our clients. Together with our partners, we managed to provide quality IT-services to businesses, organizations, and institutions. iDeal Technologies is a one-stop solution for all you IT needs. iDealTechnologies has developed the Data Management Framework to obtain a uniform view of the data, to process it in a quality-assured, clear, transparent and traceable manner. We support you in the conception, implementation, and ongoing readjustment of your individual data management strategy with the aim of a data-driven organization.
Whether it is upgrading your current website or creating a new one, we have the expertise to do so. Software product development changes continually and you must stay on top of the changes to keep your business booming.
By having multiple offices in the Balkans and in Central Europe, we have created the perfect network to be available for our clients.
Our specialization gives you the security that we will not deal with anything else, day or night. That is why we are equipped with know-how in this area that you will hardly find anywhere else in this form.
